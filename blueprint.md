# **App Name**: OneCup Elite

## Core Features:

- Tournament Hub Creator: Dynamic management module to set up multi-sport or gaming tournaments with rules, prizes, and specific schedules.
- Smart Registration & QR Entry: User-friendly registration flow for teams and individuals that automatically generates QR codes for physical access control.
- Live Prize Pool Tracker: Real-time dashboard reflecting total 'cagnotte' status and distribution tiers to build community engagement.
- Secure Omni-Channel Payments: Integration for Mobile Money and Card transactions to facilitate seamless tournament fees and ticket sales.
- Community Pulse Tool: AI-powered tool to curate community discussions, highlight trending tournament moments, and assist in content moderation.
- Live Bracket & Results: Real-time automated tournament trees and leaderboards to keep spectators and players updated.
- Command Center Admin: High-level overview for organizers to manage participants, sponsors, payments, and financial analytics.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary Color: Electric Performance Blue (#3355FF), conveying energy and professional competition.
- Background: Midnight Graphite (#10121A), a deeply desaturated blue providing a premium, focused environment.
- Accent: Deep Azure (#0088CC), an analogous hue for supportive interactive elements and high contrast against the background.
- Headline Font: 'Space Grotesk', a proportional sans-serif with a techy, scientific feel ideal for competition and scoreboards.
- Body Font: 'Inter', a neutral and highly readable grotesque sans-serif for long tournament rules and descriptions.
- Monochromatic line icons with sharp edges to match the 'Sportif' and 'Moderne' aesthetic, accented with orange glows.
- Kinetic, physics-based transitions for brackets and leaderboard updates to maintain the energetic atmosphere of the platform.
